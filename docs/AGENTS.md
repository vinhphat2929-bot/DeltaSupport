# AGENTS

## Delta One

- Day la app desktop noi bo `Delta One`.
- Frontend: `CustomTkinter`
- Backend: `FastAPI`
- Data that va log that di qua `API` roi backend ghi xuong `SQL Server`

## Luat lam viec

- uu tien sua nho, dung pham vi
- khong tu y doi topbar, window mode, landing screen
- khong tu y chinh `Lock Screen`
- khong tu y chinh function `Sync Card to Ticket` trong `SQL`
- neu user noi phan nao da on thi coi la khoa cung
- `Task Follow` hien tai phai giu UI cu trong `pages/process_page.py`
- neu lam `Task Follow`, uu tien chi doi store/cache/service; khong tu doi layout
- tra loi ve deploy phai noi dung: frontend-only thi khong can copy len may chu

## Backend va may chu

- backend trong workspace chi la ban copy local
- backend that nam tren may chu
- neu sua backend, luon phai noi ro:
  - file nao da sua local
  - file nao can copy len may chu
  - co can restart backend hay khong
